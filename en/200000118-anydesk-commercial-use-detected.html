How to Fix AnyDesk Commercial Use Detected?

  • Tracy Tran
  • 1 comments

If you’ve encountered the AnyDesk commercial use detected message, you're not alone. This article explores why this issue occurs and provides practical solutions, including the best free alternative to AnyDesk - UltraViewer.

Understanding "AnyDesk Commercial Use Detected"

Many users have turned to AnyDesk as a remote control solution due to its user-friendly interface and reliability. However, an increasing number of users are facing the frustrating “AnyDesk commercial use detected” message, which restricts their ability to use the software freely.

Why Are You Seeing AnyDesk Commercial Use Detected?

When TeamViewer started enforcing strict limitations on its free version, AnyDesk quickly became the go-to alternative for many users. However, as its popularity grew, so did the scrutiny from its system. Users might receive the following notification:

“AnyDesk commercial use detected. We detected 15+ outgoing connections related to your Client ID in the last 6 weeks. If you are using AnyDesk for Professional Technical Support, you need a license.”

Many individuals primarily use AnyDesk for personal, infrequent tasks such as remote work, installing software, or assisting family members with technical issues. Unfortunately, the system may misinterpret these activities as commercial usage, leading to sudden disconnections and restrictions on your account.

How AnyDesk Detects Commercial Use

AnyDesk utilizes advanced technology to keep tabs on how its software is used, ensuring that its licensing policies are upheld.

When you connect to remote desktops, AnyDesk carefully monitors your activity, focusing on the frequency and volume of your outgoing connections linked to your client ID. If you find yourself making more than 15 connections in a span of six weeks, the system may flag your usage as commercial. Additionally, AnyDesk's algorithms assess whether your connection patterns align with personal use—like helping a friend with a quick tech issue—or suggest a more professional application, such as providing technical support for a business.

This robust monitoring system helps AnyDesk differentiate between casual users and those utilizing the software for commercial purposes, which is why you might encounter the alert "AnyDesk commercials are detected." These measures ensure that the software remains free for personal users while encouraging those who use it for business to purchase the appropriate licenses.

Solutions for AnyDesk Commercial Use Detected Issue

If you find yourself facing the "AnyDesk commercial use detected" issue, here are several strategies to regain access without purchasing a license:

  1. Uninstall and Reinstall AnyDesk: In some cases, uninstalling and reinstalling the software can help reset your usage status. Make sure to remove any residual files before reinstalling.

  2. Limit Your Connections: Reduce the frequency of your connections to avoid being flagged for excessive use. Connecting to fewer devices can help prevent the system from misclassifying your usage.

  3. Submit a Support Request: Reach out to AnyDesk support to explain your situation. They may be able to review your account and lift the restrictions if they determine that you are using it for personal purposes.

  4. Consider Alternatives: If the restrictions persist or if you’re looking for a more flexible option, consider switching to a different remote desktop software.
